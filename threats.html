Threats

Emergencies occur daily in the UK and around the world, arising from severe weather, natural hazards, deliberate actions, accidents, or infrastructure failures. They can unfold rapidly and end within hours or persist over days, months, or even longer. Understanding these potential threats is the first step toward being prepared. From natural disasters like floods and wildfires to human-made crises such as power grid failures, economic collapses, or military conflicts, these events can significantly impact daily life, communities, and essential services. By learning about these risks and how they might affect you, along with assessing your level of risk based on your location and global conditions, you can stay informed and vigilant.
